Why Australia?

As per QS world ranking 2020 of the top 500 Universities in the world there are at least 30+ universities in Australia. The QS World University Rankings is an annual publication of university rankings by Quacquarelli Symonds (QS).

Cost of Study

Tuition fees for international students in Australia start at around $20,000 (Australian dollars) per year of study. The average fee is over $30,000. Australian universities charge tuition fees based on the number of units you take. The annual fee for a course is the cost of 2 semesters of full-time study (8 units)

Scholarships

Universities across Australia provide a wide range of merit based scholarships to international students helping them fund their studies. Australian government, public and private establishments also offer many scholarships, grants and bursaries. Most scholarship are based on Good academic standing. In some scholarships, factors like extracurricular activities, and volunteering can be advantageous.

All most all scholarships to study in Australia cover tuition fee , some may also cover living expenses. At times there is a 100% scholarships covering both tuition and living expenses for Research programs. Do note that the scholarship amount and type of award varies between institutions. Getting a scholarship is highly competitive. Reach us to help you with application well ahead in time.

Visa

With effect from July 1, 2016, a student subclass 500 visa lets you stay in Australia for the duration of your study program, which is mostly up to five years. It is your responsibility to ensure that your visa remains valid and you follow the conditions as mentioned on your student visa. You can read up in detail about this kind of visa on the Australian Government’s Department of Home Affairs website.

Stay Back Options

A Post Study Work is for international students who have completed the Australian Study Requirements and have gained either a bachelor’s, masters or a doctoral degree from a recognized Australian University. The tenure depends on the kind of degree you hold. For instance, a bachelor’s degree, a bachelor’s honors degree or a master’s degree (a degree that does not have a linked industrial training) is valid for 2 years. A Masters with Research degree qualifies you for a Temporary Visa for up to 3 years and a Doctoral Degree makes you eligible for a Temporary Visa of 4 years.

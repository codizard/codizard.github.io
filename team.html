Why study abroad?

Globalization is changing the way the world works, and employers are increasingly looking for workers who have cross-cultural competence and cutting-edge technical skills.Study abroad is a life-changing experience for many students, opening their eyes to different ways of life and promoting understanding and tolerance. By looking at research on the more directly quantifiable aspects of study abroad impact, studies show students who study abroad have better grades, experience less attrition, and graduate from college at higher rates than students who do not study abroad.Also Employers value study abroad experiences in the workplace.

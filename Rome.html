BBA/MBA Students

Digital Management – Key to Innovation and performance is a 2 weeks summer program will focus on the following subjects

✓Digital Management

✓Business analytics

✓Digital Marketing

✓Supply chain Management

✓Strategic Leadership and Change Management

Dates : 15th June 2020 to 27th June 2020

Course Focus - The Program will focus on new models for innovation and project management performance and digital management methods are imparted during this intensive program. Students learn about methods and related concepts within the field of digital management and seminars will be held on Business analytics, Supply chain Management, Strategic Leadership and Change Management.Strategic Leadership and Change Management It will equip and enhance student’s theoretical knowledge with required tools and techniques and thereby giving them an edge over others on the global job market competition with real time case studies.

Program Outcome - Students benefit from the program on how Digital Management has the potential to integrate and streamline entire business process in order to increase business performance and Increase productivity.

Business Analytics will equip students with tools that empowers businesses to make quick, informed decisions thereby increasing productivity Supply Chain helps in companies gain competitive edge, many global companies are recognising and adopting the expanding role of digitalisation as a fundamental Business strategy to realise innovation. Agile innovation methods is the Mantra for Learning by doing, Rapidly delivering working products, developing innovative delivery methods and adapting to changing requirements.

Who can attend? The summer program is for students studying in BBA/MBA, with focus on Management as specialization.

Program Cost: Non-EU students- Total program 2800 Euros. This includes Flight, visa fee, travel in Rome, company visit travel cost, accommodation(shared) and airport transfer. (Fees does not include food). Students are accepted only through universities, individual students are not accepted.

Engineering Students

Applying Mechatronics and ICT towards Innovation in real-life (AMIIRL)

5 days intensive workshop course in class. ❯1 day cultural exchange program ❯2 days Industrial visit ❯2 days sight seeing 2 days sight seeing in France and Switzerland (optional subject to number of students)

The following study modules in Mechatronics sector are: ❯Control systems for automotive and robotics ❯Internal combustion engines ❯Innovative materials for mechatronics ❯Nanotechnologies ❯Radiocommunications for Internet of Things ❯Embedded systems for mechatronics

Dates : 18th May 2020 to 31st May 2020

Program Outcome - Summer school in Mechatronics and ICT aims to provide an intensive knowledge of the topics covered in the summer program. The students can also have an awareness of the type of skills that can be acquired by enrolling in the degree of Mechatronics Engineering and in ICT and Internet Engineering. The school will be open to all interested students, who can also be interested in continuing their education at our University.

Course Structure - The course will last 2 weeks. The first Week is dedicated to the actual teaching activity, while the second one carries out visits to companies in the sectors of interest and the development of simple individual projects. The 5 days of teaching activities would include a good percentage of practical activities(laboratory demonstrations). Three days will be devoted to Mechatronics while two days will be for ICT. The teaching days include 8 hours of lessons / practical activities, 4 in the morning and 4 in the afternoon. For each of the 6 courses planned for Mechatronics, we foresee 2 blocks of 2 hours, one in the morning (for any necessary hints of theory) and one in the afternoon (basically for practical / experimental sessions). As for ICT, the 2-day teaching will be divided into theoretical courses of 2 hours in the morning and practical classes in laboratories in the afternoon.

Key Feature: Industry visit to Maserati and Fiat

Who can attend? The summer program is for students studying in Mechanical, Materials Science, Mechtronics, Robotics and allied majors.

Program Cost (Non-EU students): Total program cost 2600 Euros. This includes Flight, visa fee, travel in Rome, company visit travel cost, accommodation(shard) and airport transfer.Fee does not include Food.Students are accepted only through universities, individual students are not accepted.

Contact :For further information and registration please contact Dr. Sunil Kumar,tarangapade@economia.uniroma2.it

Who are we?

GoWorld aims to break down the perceptions that study abroad is expensive. There are a number of scholarships offered by schools, universities, and external agencies that can help your dream of studying abroad affordable. We are educational consultants who provide you guidance on choosing the right academic career along with the valuable last-mile connection to help you land internships and jobs. We give you a holistic view of the best academic path you can take based on your profile to help you forge your career
